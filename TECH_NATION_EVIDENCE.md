## 2. Problem Being Addressed (Market Need)

Enterprise organisations face persistent challenges in risk management:

- Operational data exists in large volumes but lacks actionable insight  
- Risk scores are often opaque and non-explainable  
- Audit, compliance, and leadership teams require **narrative justification**, not raw metrics  
- Existing tools prioritise dashboards over decision support  

This creates a gap between **data availability** and **risk-informed decision-making**.

## 4. System Architecture & Workflow

**Input:**  
- CSV files containing operational events, incidents, or logs  

**Processing Pipeline:**  
1. Data validation and cleansing  
2. Feature aggregation and enrichment  
3. Key Risk Indicator (KRI) computation  
4. Statistical anomaly estimation  
5. Composite enterprise risk score generation  
6. Automated plain-English risk narrative  

**Output:**  
- Overall risk score (0â€“100)  
- Risk classification (Low / Medium / High)  
- KRI breakdown  
- Explainable drivers of risk  
- Executive-ready narrative report  
- Downloadable scored dataset  

This full pipeline is demonstrable via the live system.
